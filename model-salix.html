Initial (Hand-Made) Markov Logic Model for the Salix Dataset

Types: Probabilistic, First-Order Logic.

Domains

  1. location
  2. species

Predicates

  1. HotTemperature(location)
  2. Presence(species, location)
  3. IsPlant(species)
  4. IsHerbivore(species)
  5. IsParasite(species)
  6. CloselyRelated(species, species)
  7. PreyOnAt(species, species, location)
  8. PreyOn(species, species)
  9. Covariation(species, species)

Rules

  1. \(\neg PreyOnAt(species_x, species_y, location)\)
  2. \(\neg PreyOnAt(species_x, species_x, location)\)
  3. \(IsHerbivore(species_x) \land PreyOnAt(species_x, species_y, location) \Rightarrow IsPlant(species_y).\)
  4. \(IsParasite(species_x) \land PreyOnAt(species_x, species_y, location) \Rightarrow IsHerbivore(species_y).\)
  5. \(IsPlant(species_x) \Rightarrow \neg PreyOnAt(species_x, species_y, location).\)
  6. \(CloselyRelated(species_x, species_y) \land PreyOn(species_x, species_z) \Rightarrow PreyOn(species_y, species_z).\)
  7. \(PreyOn(species_x, species_y) \leftrightarrow Covariation(species_x, species_y).\)
  8. \(PreyOn(species_x, spexies_y) \leftrightarrow !Covariation(species_x, species_y).\)
