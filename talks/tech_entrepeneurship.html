The only solution on the market that will:

  • Adapt to optimize long-term objectives.
  • Require almost no maintenance from you.

What's based wrong with the current DSP technology:

  • Based entirely on unreliable cookie technology that inserts or extrapolates inaccurate information into cookies (For example, 33Across assumes if you and I email each other, I buy diapers for my kids and you probably need it too!).
  • Unable to adapt to changing user interests.

Based on modern Artificial Intelligence technology:

  • Our technology is based on Reinforcement Learning, a technique widely used in robotics
  • Inherent exploration mechanisms allows the algorithms to adapt to changing user interests, as well as validating the values of the input data (e.g. cookie).
  • It is an A.I. technology that can make sub-optimal decision at times in order to achieve long-term objectives (e.g. sacrifice a piece to win the game in the chess).

Benefits to the end users:

  • Almost no maintenance needed therefore saves you precious time to focus on your own core technology.
  • Machine makes intelligent decisions for you therefore it saves the costs of expensive data scientists and campaign managers to interpret the results and make adjustments based on the same learning process that the machine is already capable.

Will it slow your website?

No! Modern cloud infrastructures are fast enough to support databases as a service (e.g.: MongoLab, Azure, ...)
